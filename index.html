Editions of Grimm's Fairy Tales:

First Edition

1812 - First Edition of Grimm's Fairy Tales- VOL1 contained 86 Stories

1815 - First Edition of Grimm's Fairy Tales- VOL2 contains 70 stories

Second Edition

1819 - Second Edition of Grimm's Fairy Tales- Volumes 1 & 2.

1822 - Second Edition of Grimm's Fairy Tales- Volume 3. With the third volume the total number of stories was at 170.

Third Edition

1837 - Third Edition of Grimm's Fairy Tales.

Fourth Edition

1840 - Fourth Edition of Grimm's Fairy Tales.

Fifth Edition

1843 - Fifth Edition of Grimm's Fairy Tales.

Sixth Edition

1850 - Sixth Edition of Grimm's Fairy Tales.

Seventh Edition

1857 - Seventh Edition of Grimm's Fairy Tales. This edition held 211 stories.
